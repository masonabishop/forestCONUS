US Forest Cover Map

This map visualizes forest cover percentages across U.S. counties from 2001 through 2021. As urban sprawl exapnds, we might expect a decline in forest canopies. However, with initiatives like Trees Lexington and Reforest the Bluegrass in Lexington, KY, dedicated to growing urban forests, do we instead observe an increase in tree canopy over time?
